*** ETHICAL HACKING NOTES ***
Author: Kripa SB

1. Phases of Penetration Testing
   - Reconnaissance: Gathering OSINT.
   - Scanning: Finding open ports and vulnerabilities.
   - Exploitation: Gaining unauthorized access.
   - Post-Exploitation: Maintaining access and pivoting.
   - Reporting: Documenting findings for the client.

2. Legal and Ethical Rules
   - Always obtain written consent (Rules of Engagement).
   - Stay within the defined scope.
   - Do no harm to production environments.
