*** CYBERSECURITY CONCEPTS ***
Author: Kripa SB

1. The Security Triad (CIA)
   - Confidentiality: Data privacy and encryption.
   - Integrity: Data accuracy and tamper-proofing.
   - Availability: System uptime and accessibility.

2. Common Threat Vectors
   - Phishing: Deceptive communications.
   - Malware: Malicious software (ransomware, spyware).
   - SQLi: Database injection attacks.

3. Defense Mechanisms
   - Firewalls: Traffic filtering.
   - IDS/IPS: Intrusion detection and prevention.
   - Cryptography: Symmetric and asymmetric encryption.
